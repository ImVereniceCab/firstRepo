In the beginning I was afraid to meet new people in a new school but when I did it felt so relief that I wasn't the only one that was new to the school. I would say that knowing that everyone comes from differnet background, really helped me feel comforable in school. One thing that is challenging when going to school is transportantion. I live in Staten Island, so when I go to City Tech I have to take a ferry over to the city and train.

But looking from today I can say that try new things even when it dosen't feel possible. Like the quote Nike says, Just Do it. This has always been my go to quote because it is easy to remember.

I would like to share that my favorite shows/movie is anything that has magic, heros, or anything that isn't natural. The supernatural is so cool to think about, because it draws you out of the real world. If I were to have superpower it would be teleportation or anyting that don't require me walking long distance.
